Experience

Master of Engineering - UGM

Graduated from Universitas Gadjah Mada in Geomatics Engineering. My thesis, titled "Development of Spatial Based Agricultural Irrigation Management Information System", focused on creating an innovative spatial-based system to optimize irrigation management in agricultural domains.

Remote Sensing and Climate Lead - Koltiva AG

At Koltiva AG, I lead remote sensing and climate projects to support climate change analysis and environmental monitoring. I collaborate with cross-functional teams to design and implement innovative climate solutions, integrating remote sensing data with climate models to assess and predict environmental impacts, ensuring compliance with environmental standards and regulations.

GIS Developer - World Resources Institute

As a GIS Developer at WRI, I worked on translating business needs into technical requirements and specifications. I developed and deployed automated prioritization scripts and participated in the design, development, and implementation of complex geospatial applications, supporting sustainable commodities research and delivering high-quality GIS solutions.
